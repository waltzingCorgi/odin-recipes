Pumpkin Bread

Description

Pumpkin bread from Budget Bytes. Who doesn't like the autumn spirit in breaf form?

Ingredients

  • 15oz pumpkin puree
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2/3 cups oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2/3 cups water
  • 4 cups sifted flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2tsp pumpkin spice
  • 1 tsp butter, for greasing

Instructions

  1. Place a rack in the middle of your oven and preheat it to 350Â°F. Grease 2 loaf pans with butter. In a medium bowl, mix the sifted flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and pumpkin spice.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the pumpkin puree, sugar, oil, eggs, vanilla, and water.
  3. Add the dry ingredients on top of the wet ingredients.
  4. Split the batter between the two greased loaf pans and smooth the top.
  5. Bake for about an hour, or until a knife inserted into the crack in the loaf's top crust hits the bottom of the pan and comes out clean.
